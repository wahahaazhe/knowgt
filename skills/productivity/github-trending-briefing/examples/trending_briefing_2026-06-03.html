What is it? A data pipeline from Microsoft that extracts entities, relationships, and knowledge graphs from raw documents — then uses those graphs to make LLM answers more accurate and grounded.
Simple analogy It's like giving an LLM a well-organized index of a textbook instead of dumping the whole book on its desk. The index helps it find the right pages instead of flipping through everything.
What can it help you do?
  • Help you build Q&A systems that actually cite the right source documents
  • Let you connect scattered information across thousands of internal docs automatically
  • Save you from LLMs that confidently make up answers when the real info exists in your files
Who actually needs it? Enterprise architect building internal knowledge base search Data engineer tired of chunk-and-embed pipelines that miss connections Legal tech PM needing accurate document retrieval for compliance
